Types of Tense



  • Past Tense
    • Past Simple
      • Example:I played football.
    • Past Perfect
      • Example:I had played football.
    • Past Continuous
      • Example:I was playing football.
    • Past Perfect Continuous
      • Example:I had playing football for 3 years.
  • Present Tense
    • Present Simple
      • Example:I play cricket every
    • Present Perfect
      • Example:i have just played crickrt.
    • Present Continuous
      • Example:I am playing cricket.
    • Present Perfect Continuous
      • Example:I have been playing cricket for 3 years.
  • Future Tense
    • Future Simple
      • Example:I will play football.
    • Future Perfect
      • Example:I will have played football.
    • Future Continuous
      • Example:I will be playing football next sunday.
    • Future Perfect Continuous
      • Example:I would have been playing football for 3 yrars
